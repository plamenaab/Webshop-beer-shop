PAULANER

„Gut, besser, Paulaner“

The full enjoyment of wheat beer meets the relaxed, light Munich lifestyle: Our Isar Weisse is full of flavor - and that with only 3.4% alcohol. Aromatic in character and at the same time so wonderfully refreshing and carefree that you would be happy to order a second beer. The special hop variety Citra gives our wheat beer a pleasantly fruity note that unfolds more and more with every sip. This fresh lightness complements the palate perfectly with the distinctive aroma of our special malts.

ALCOHOL: 3,4 % vol

Size: 33cl
